Our Purpose & Mission

"Serving God, Loving People, Changing Lives."

At Living Waters, our doctrine defines our focus and forms the foundation for how we minister, assemble, and care for our surrounding community. We stand firmly on biblical truths:

The Word of God

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and completely authoritative Word of God, serving as our ultimate guide for faith and life layout rules.

Community Transformation

We believe the local church should actively serve as an anchor of hope and care, displaying Christ's love through tangible community outreach actions.
