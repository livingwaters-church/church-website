Why Connection Matters

Sundays are wonderful for assembling together as a whole church family, but spiritual growth happens best when we connect in smaller circles. Connection Groups are small groups of people who meet regularly to unpack scripture, discuss practical life application, pray together, and support one another through life's ups and downs.

Our Mid-Week Discipleship

Wednesday Evening Bible Study (5:00 PM): Our central mid-week interactive study assembly. We gather together to dive deeper into corporate scripture series, running concurrently with our youth and children's discipleship tracks.

Home Connection Groups: Varied seasonal small groups meeting in local living rooms throughout the Oak Grove and surrounding areas for community fellowship and topical study studies.
