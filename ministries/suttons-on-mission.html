Church Planting in Utah

The Sutton family serves as our primary missionary partners on the ground in Utah—one of the least evangelical states in the country. From supporting local works like Redemption Bear River in Tremonton to organizing community group outreaches in Syracuse, Living Waters stands directly with them through prayer, short-term team deployments, and financial funding.
