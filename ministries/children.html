What We Value

We believe learning about God should be the highlight of your child's week! Our programs are fully tailored to be age-appropriate, interactive, and safe. Every worker completes a background screening process to ensure a secure environment for your family.

Weekly Schedule & Meeting Times

Sunday Mornings (10:30 AM): Children's Church & Nursery care run concurrently with our main corporate worship gathering.

Wednesday Evenings (5:00 PM): Mid-week discipleship programs packed with games, biblical lessons, and small group memory verse activities.
