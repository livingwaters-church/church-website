As Iron Sharpens Iron

Gladiator Training is the core vehicle for men's discipleship at Living Waters. We are focused on building godly men who lead their families with integrity, hold one another accountable, and stand firm in their faith. This isn't just about casual gatherings—it's about intentional training for life's battles.

Gatherings & Meeting Windows

Gladiator Training Gathering: Meets the 1st Saturday of each month @ 9:00 AM. This is a Bring Your Own Breakfast (BYOB) setup—bring your favorite breakfast, pull up a chair, and jump straight into the Word and table discussions.

Service & Action Projects: Periodic hands-on deployment teams assisting with building maintenance, community work days, and emergency assistance projects.
