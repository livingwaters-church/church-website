What We Are About

Our student ministry is a high-energy environment built specifically for middle and high school students. We tackle real-world topics through biblical teaching, cultivate deep discussions in small groups, and coordinate regular fun events, retreats, and service opportunities.

Weekly Gathering Times

Sunday Mornings (09:00 AM): Youth meet in the annex building.

Wednesday Nights (5:00 PM): The main youth gathering! Mid-week services feature dynamic games, corporate worship, practical messages, and small group circles.
