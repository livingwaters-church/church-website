Community & Growth

The Women's Ministry at Living Waters is designed to connect women of all ages and stages of life. Through targeted seasonal Bible studies, regular fellowship events, and sweet intentional prayer groupings, we strive to build deep roots that help us grow in Christ and serve our homes and community well.

Gatherings & Seasonal Schedules

Seasonal Bible Studies: Small group studies run on monday evenings throughout the spring and fall semesters.

Fellowship Nights: Periodic dynamic gatherings, dinners, and mid-morning brunches scheduled throughout the calendar year to relax and connect.
